[Renal/Metformin]
IF patient is on Metformin AND (Creatinine > 1.4 OR eGFR < 30 OR "renal failure" is mentioned):
  RISK: Lactic Acidosis.
  REVIEW: Verify Metformin contraindications.

[Hyperkalemia]
IF Potassium (K) > 5.0:
  RISK: Hyperkalemia.
  REVIEW: Check for ACE inhibitors, ARBs, or Spironolactone.
IF Potassium (K) > 5.5:
  RISK: Critical Hyperkalemia.
  REVIEW: Immediate clinical review required.

[Bradycardia/Beta-Blockers]
IF Heart Rate < 60 AND patient is on Beta-Blocker (Metoprolol, Atenolol, Carvedilol):
  RISK: Symptomatic Bradycardia.
  REVIEW: Assess for dizziness/syncope.

[Bleeding Risk]
IF patient is on Warfarin/Eliquis/Xarelto AND (INR > 3.0 OR "bleeding" mentioned):
  RISK: Hemorrhage.
  REVIEW: Verify dosing and INR targets.

[Antibiotics/Allergy]
IF patient has Penicillin Allergy AND prescribed (Penicillin, Amoxicillin, Ampicillin, Augmentin):
  RISK: Anaphylaxis.
  REVIEW: Verify allergy history and reaction severity.
